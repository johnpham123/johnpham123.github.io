**** HOW TO PLAY ****:

  Use left mouse to click menu buttons.
  Use keyboards to play the game. Key controls:
     - w: jump
     - a: back
     - s: squat
     - d: forward

     - u: light punch
     - i: light kick
     - o: power burst
     - j: strong punch
     - k: strong kick
     - l: emergency escape

 * Basic Actions:
     - Dash: Press forward twice
     - Step back: Press backward twice
     - Defend: Press backward while opponent attacking
     - Squat defend: Press backward and squat
     - Ducking: Emergency escape: press emergency escape key
     - Strong hit: Press punch and kick at once
     - Power burst: Press power key when you have 3 power points

 * Power Burst:
       There are 3 types of power burt:
       Power Mode, Skill Mode and Defend Mode
 
       In following conditions, you can trigger power burst:
       1. You have 3 power points
       2. Your blood bar blinks and you have at least 1 power point

       Functions of power burst:
       1. You can use potential skills, each character has 2 potential skills.
         You can launch potential skills by 2 ways:
         Skill1: S + D + power key
         Skill2: S + A + power key
       2. In power burst mode, characters have some special effects.

       Power Mode (Press power burst key to trigger)

       Functions:
       1. Super skills turns to Max super skills
       2. Damage increasing by 10%

       Skill Mode (Press power burst key and forward to trigger)

       Functions:
       1. Being able to use “Super Cancel”, you can start skills before last skill ending.
       2. Being able to trigger emergency escape after defending opponent’s attack.

       Defend Mode (Press power burst key and backward to trigger)

       Functions:
       1. Body becomes “hard”, you can defend 1 hit while you are attacking.
       2. Your life bar will not decrease while defend.
       3. You can use “Strong Hit” (P + K) to knock opponent away while defending.
       4. Your damage to the enemy will decrease by 20%.
